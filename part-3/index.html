The Reconstruction Manual

“Trust, Context, and Intuition.” Three pages smuggled into Out of Office, 2014. Editor: “too abstract.” Ten years later, those three words explain everything.

The irreducible trinity of knowledge work, broken everywhere, rebuilt here. Not through transformation theater but patient reconstruction. Tools, not theology. Practices, not prescriptions.

The Tools That Work
